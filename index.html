About Us

Tick Construction Ltd, officially registered in May 2025, is a forward-thinking and dynamic construction company. We are founded on the seasoned experience of four professional engineers, each bringing a wealth of hands-on expertise in building design, site management, and civil infrastructure.

We are committed to delivering exceptional construction solutions that are grounded in technical excellence, professional integrity, and client-centered service. From residential projects and commercial buildings to renovations and infrastructure, our team is equipped to handle each phase with precision and accountability.
